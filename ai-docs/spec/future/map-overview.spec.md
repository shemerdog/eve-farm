# Map Overview

## Overview

The map is the main play space. It lets players view their farm, expand into nearby tiles, and navigate the world with a smooth camera.

## Sub-Features

- Camera (center, pan, zoom; later: rotate, tilt)
- Tiles (grid, locked/unlocked, fog, purchase)
- Farm Tile (hosts the farm and crop interactions)

## Advanced (Not Near-Term)

- Camera rotate and tilt
- Multiple biome tile types
- Larger world layers or regions

When any advanced item becomes a real feature, create its spec in `spec/future/advanced/`.
