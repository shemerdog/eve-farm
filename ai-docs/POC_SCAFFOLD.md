# Heritage Farm Game — POC Scaffolding (Concise)

## 1) Snapshot
- **One‑liner:** A Township‑style farming game that reconnects players with ancient Jewish/Israelite life through casual farming and ethical dilemmas.
- **Why it exists:** Make cultural heritage approachable and inclusive, without strict religious framing.
- **Tone:** Warm, welcoming, non‑dogmatic.

## 2) Target & Platform
- **Audience:** Casual gamers; heritage‑interested players; broad age range.
- **Platform (POC):** Mobile‑first design (works on desktop later).

## 3) Core Loop (POC)
1. Plant crop (wheat).
2. Wait for growth timer.
3. Harvest crop.
4. Face a dilemma.
5. Allocate outcome → resources + Devotion/Morality/Faithful meter.

## 4) Unique Hook
- Cultural dilemmas based on commandments (e.g., פאה, tithes).
- Optional “religious” vs “national” terminology for the same concept.

## 5) MVP / POC Scope (Must‑Have)
- 4 land plots.
- Wheat planting → growth → harvest.
- Growth timer and basic UI state changes.
- Dilemma 1: Leave part of crops for the poor (מצוות פאה).
- Dilemma 2: Tithes allocation: community teachings/Levi 10%, poor 1–3%, worship 1%.
- Player keeps remainder.
- Devotion/Morality/Faithful meter always visible and affected by choices.

## 6) Excluded From POC (Later)
- Selling crops, money, buying land.
- Additional crops/animals/buildings.
- Production chains, co‑op/social, events.
- Cosmetics store.

## 7) Art & Audio Direction
- **Art:** Cute, animated, comic‑style characters and world; grounded realism.
- **Audio:** Calm, warm, subtle folkloric touches.

## 8) Monetization (Later, Non‑Pay‑to‑Win)
- Cosmetic packs only (farm skins, building styles, outfits).

## 9) POC Success Criteria (1‑Hour Target)
- Player can complete a full wheat cycle on 4 plots.
- At least 1 dilemma triggers and affects the meter.
- UI clearly shows crops, timers, and meter changes.
